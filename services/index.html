Our services

How effective physiotherapy is lies in the accuracy of the assessment. Revival Physiotherapy carries out detailed initial assessments in order to provide a diagnosis and realistic and achievable outcome goals.

This will be coupled with education, home care and exercises.

Following the assessment, hands-on physiotherapy treatment will be provided which can include:

  • Soft tissue release / transverse frictions
  • Electrotherapy
  • Joint mobilisation techniques
  • PNF, passive and active-assisted movements, stretches

We assess & treat

Revival Physiotherapy can provide treatment and management of many musculoskeletal conditions including:

  • Joint pain / strain (shoulder, elbow, wrist, hands, hips, knees, ankles, feet)
  • Muscle strain / tear
  • Tendinopathy / RSI (tennis & golfer’s elbow, achilles tendonitis)
  • Neck & back pain (including acute / early intervention, wear and tear, reduce core strength & disc dysfunction)
  • General deconditioning or reduced movement
  • Arthritis
  • Whiplash
  • Nerve pain (e.g. sciatica)
  • Biomechanical issues
  • Plantar fasciitis
  • Mobility / walking difficulties
  • Pre / post-surgery rehabilitation

Occupational Health services

Caroline has 15 years’ experience working in in a wide variety of occupational physiotherapy settings and is a member of ACPOHE (Association of Chartered Physiotherapists in Occupational Health and Ergonomics). Caroline has worked as a Senior Physiotherapist providing:

  • Occupational Health Physiotherapy Assessment (fitness for work / injury management)
  • Sickness management for musculoskeletal conditions or injuries
  • DSE / workstation assessment
  • Manual handling assessment and training
  • Ergonomic assessment

If you are unsure if physiotherapy can help you please contact us by telephone or email.
