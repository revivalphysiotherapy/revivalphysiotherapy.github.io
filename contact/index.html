Covid-19 information

The Covid Pandemic has affected Private Physiotherapy provision significantly. The lockdown period prevented many of us working. During this time, to provide me with sufficient work, I am working with Willaston Physiotherapy in Bromborough for a large amount of my available hours. Willaston is a spacious clinic where social distancing and strict hygiene can be adhered to easily.

If you are able to travel and you’d like to see me for an appointment I would be very grateful for your loyalty if you were able to contact Willaston Physio and Sports Rehab on 0151 327 3361 to book an appointment.

If you would like to discuss home visit appointment options please do get in touch with me below and I would be very happy to help. There are less home visits appointments available due to the difficulties the pandemic creates.
