About Revival Physiotherapy

Caroline Everett Physiotherapist

Revival Physiotherapy’s lead physiotherapist, Caroline Everett, graduated in 2001 from Sheffield Hallam University with a BSc (hons) Physiotherapy.

With 15 years in the private sector, Caroline has committed to a very hands on treatment approach and detailed assessment process.

Caroline’s methodical approach ensures clients understand their injury / condition, receive treatment focused on accurate timescales and are provided with detailed home care and exercise guidance.

Caroline’s caring nature and client focus is the underpinning base of Revival Physiotherapy’s ethos to improve the condition or strength of clients.
